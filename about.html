About

I am currently a computer science student at the University of Texas at Austin graduating in the Spring of 2014. I was born and raised in the small town of Graham, Texas. I currently reside in Austin and would love to begin my career here. While I have an interest in a wide variety of fields in CS, the focus in my coursework has been in classes focused in software engineering, mobile computing, and data analytics. I love computer science because it is the perfect balance of theory and application; You can apply, implement, and reuse any concept without needing any other physical resources other than a computer. I like to think of programming as putting a jigsaw puzzle together. You take bunch of small pieces and fit them together to make a picture. While the jigsaw puzzle has defined pieces and only one solution, it is the programmers job to manually define (or reuse) each piece to find a way to put the picture together.
